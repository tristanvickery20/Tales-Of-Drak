# Vision

Tales of Drak aims to become a dark fantasy co-op RPG framework that is readable, moddable, and expandable.

The near-term vision is not a giant content dump. It is a strong gameplay skeleton that can support:

- class-based characters
- progression and equipment
- gathering and crafting loops
- housing and snapping build pieces
- pets and mounts
- 4–6 player dungeon runs

The framework should feel small and understandable at first, then scale outward through data and modular systems.
