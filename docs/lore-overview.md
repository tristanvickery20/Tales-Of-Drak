# Lore Overview

Lore should stay skeletal during the framework stage.

Starter premise:

- the world is old, fractured, and marked by the remains of earlier powers
- ruins, keeps, caverns, and blighted wilds fit the tone
- factions, gods, and historical detail should stay light for now
- names and terms should support dark fantasy mood without locking the setting too early

Early content should use placeholders where possible so the framework can mature before the setting hardens.
