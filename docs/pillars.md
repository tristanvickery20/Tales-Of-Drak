# Pillars

## 1. Dark fantasy tone
The world should feel old, dangerous, grounded, and slightly mournful rather than loud or cartoonish.

## 2. Co-op adventure
Small-group play matters more than mass concurrency. The target is 4–6 players, not thousands.

## 3. Character expression
Classes, subclasses, species, gear, pets, mounts, and housing should all contribute to player identity.

## 4. Useful world interaction
Gathering, crafting, and building should not feel like side systems glued on after combat.

## 5. Modular growth
Every major system should be able to start simple and grow without requiring a rewrite.
