# Art Direction

The project should lean into restrained dark fantasy.

Starter direction:

- muted colors over saturated palettes
- rugged materials such as stone, iron, aged wood, bone, hide, and dim cloth
- silhouettes should be readable at gameplay distance
- avoid overly flashy high-fantasy excess in the early visual language
- interfaces should feel clean and functional, not ornate to the point of confusion

This file is intentionally brief until the gameplay skeleton is stronger.
